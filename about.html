About Night Sky

Night Sky is a Producer, DJ and Multi Instrumentalist based in Santa Cruz, California. Growing up with a musical upbringing, he played multiple instruments from an early age and soon found himself drawn to music production. His unique melodic sound and production style draws inspiration from across the entire spectrum of music and relies heavily on a combination of organic instruments such as electric guitars, pianos and electric basses and powerful synths. Amongst his biggest musical influences is a lineup of musical heavyweights such ILLENIUM, Seven Lions, Midnight Kids, Soar, Man Cub and Angels & Airwaves. His music has already garnered support from artists and tastemakers alike, chief amongst them Midnight Kids and Airwave Music.
